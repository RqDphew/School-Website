About us

History

The beginnings of the teaching-educational process in the school date back to 1958, based on на the decision made on 7-X-1957 to establish an industrial school with a practical training in Bitola, with the task of preparing skilled workers for the needs of industry through two professions - mechanical and electrical with a duration of three years and a capacity of 300 students. As the needs for professional staff in the Bitola economy grew, the establishment of Technical School which started working in 1960, and soon after Technical School for adults and the school for highly skilled workers (master school). This school is formed according to the need and request of the then factory for refrigerators Bitola. As further grew up the needs of the economy, so the school expanded and developed. In 1961 in within the school is established and a higher technical school. It later stands out as special educational institution, Technical Faculty, within the University "St. Kliment Ohridski" from Bitola.



The development of industry in Bitola required new staff who were not trained in any high school in Bitola. Therefore, in the school year 1967/68, the expansion of the structural types of education in the school began and a department for low current was formed for qualified mechanics for radio-television and electroacoustics.

Also based on the growing needs of professionals in the textile industry in Bitola, a textile school was opened within the Center.

The needs for professional staff for the economy have necessitated the need for training of expert teaching staff. That is why, within the school, an emergency center is being established studying at the Higher Vocational Pedagogical School from Rijeka-Republic of Croatia. The gradual formation of the mentioned schools, schools and centers and their concentration in the building of the industrial school was a prerequisite for the establishment of a new educational institution of professional staff, electro-mechanical school center.



This school center has never been a mechanical sum of several types and shapes of professionally education, but an organizational whole in which it was established internally functionally unity and in which staff from several levels of vocational education were trained. Like that, Industrial, Technical, Technical for Adults, School for highly qualified workers, the Higher Technical School and the Teaching Center for Part-time Study VPSH-Rijeka are conceived and grew into a new educational institution EMUC "Gjuro Salaj" - Bitola. His task was for the needs of the economy to educate and train:

  1. Skilled workers in the field of metal and electrical engineering, through industrial school with practical training.
  2. Technicians and drive technicians through the electromechanical technical school and the technical adult school.
  3. Highly skilled workers in the metal and electrical engineering, through the school for highly qualified workers - master school.
  4. Senior technicians (propulsion engineers) in mechanical and electrical engineering, through the higher technical school.
  5. Vocational teachers, for vocational subjects and practical teaching of mechanical and electrical engineering through the teaching center for part-time studies at the Higher Education Institution - Rijeka.

In 1997 the school was renamed DSEMU "Gjorgji Naumov", and in 2006, by entering force of the new law on local self-government, in the Municipal Secondary Technical School or abbreviated SOTU "Gjorgji Naumov".

Today, the School follows the modern trends for education of quality staff from electrical engineering and mechanical engineering. One of those novelties is the introduction of dual professional education starting from the academic year 2019/2020.
