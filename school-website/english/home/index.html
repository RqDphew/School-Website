29. 03. 2022

Yesterday in our school was held a lecture on "Taxes in RSM". The lecture was intended for final year students, and was conducted by Biljana Stojanovska from the Regional Directorate of the Public Revenue Office - Bitola. The purpose of this event is to introduce graduates to the tax obligations of individuals and legal entities in our country which is in accordance with the curriculum for the subject Business.

Regional competitions at electrical, mechanical and traffic high schools were held on Friday (March 25th). For our region, the host was OEMUC "St. Naum Ohridski ”- Ohrid. We congratulate them on their success and wish them good luck in the state competition.

22. 03. 2022

The state competition in informatics was held on Saturday, March 19 at three locations: in the high school "Dobri Daskalov" - Kavadarci, "Semos education" - Skopje and the Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering (FINKI) - Skopje. The competition was attended by students who had previously achieved success in regional competitions. The representative from our school was the student Hristijan Nikolovski from the third year under the mentorship of Professor Marija Kolevska. In a very strong competition, Christian achieved great success by winning the third prize. With that, he once again confirmed that SOTU "Gjorgji Naumov" is a nursery of talents in the field of programming and computer science. Well done to Christian, well done to the mentor! May your rewards and successes be multiplied!
